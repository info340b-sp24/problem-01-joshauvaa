Sokka

Sokka is one of the main characters in the animated Nickelodeon series Avatar: The Last Airbender. He hails from the Southern Watertribe and has a reputation for being sarcastic, funny, and skilled with weapons including the sword and the boomerang. Sokka played a key role in Team Avatar who helped put an end to the 100 year war. He has a younger sister named Katara and had multiple love interests throughout the series including Yue and Suki. A few reasons I admire Sokka include:

  • He took on the heavy responsibility of looking after his tribe at the age of 16 after all the the men went off to fight in the war.
  • Despite being the only one in Team Avatar incapable of bending an element, he made a notable impact on their mission via his intellect and combat skills.
  • He was versatile, finding an almost perfect balance between stern leadership and comic relief.
